Welcome to AIEE

Empowering the next generation of change-makers and future leaders. Breaking limits. Building futures. Learning without boundaries. Learn, Think, Create, Lead.

Education system of Bangladesh has been largely urban-centric which has led to a great gap in the urban and rural learners. Rural and sub-district (upazila) students tend to experience significant discriminations since they have little access to formal guidance, career mentorship, and quality academic and career-related data. This in turn means that a significant proportion of students cannot make sound choices on how to select subjects, their route of higher education and long term career. The number of students which leaves their education at the Secondary School Certificate (SSC) and Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) levels either forever, or not yet knowing or having their career paths is estimated at 500,000-600,000 each year. This does not only apply to the individual learners but also on long term challenges of human resource development in the country. These systemic gaps are the key focus of AIEE (Academy of Information, Education and Entrepreneurship) because the primary aim of the organization is to fill these gaps by providing timely, precise, and systematic mentor-based instructions to students in all regions of the nation. By taking an integrated approach, which is a combination of academic support, career counseling, and skill-oriented mentorship, AIEE intends to enable students to be clear, confident, and directional.
Expected Impact: Through the interventions of AIEE, students will be predicted to become better self-confident, have better capabilities to make decisions, and arrive at a higher level of self-assessment to find, seek, and capitalize on their personal potential and the existing educational and career opportunities. After all, this project helps to lessen the disparity in education and create a more knowledgeable, competent, and future-oriented generation.

About AIEE

Philosophy and Core Principles

AIEE is built on the belief that education is a fundamental right and must be accessible to every student, regardless of geographic or socioeconomic background. The academy promotes equal educational opportunities for urban and rural learners through structured guidance and mentorship. Beyond academic instruction, AIEE emphasizes decision-making, leadership, communication, and essential life skills to help students make informed academic and career choices. It also focuses on developing critical thinking, problem-solving abilities, creativity, and an entrepreneurial mindset. Through this holistic approach, AIEE prepares students not only for academic success but also for long-term personal growth, adaptability, and responsible participation in society.

Education Reality and Current EdTech Gaps in Bangladesh

Each year, approximately 500,000–600,000 SSC and HSC students in Bangladesh discontinue their education or become academically disengaged due to the absence of proper guidance and mentorship. Many students lack awareness of which subjects and skills are relevant for future careers, while parents remain uncertain about subject selection, admission procedures, and financial planning. As a result, students face frustration, make incorrect career decisions, and lose valuable opportunities. Existing EdTech platforms largely focus on exam preparation, rely heavily on video-based content, offer minimal mentor support, and remain urban-centric, leaving rural learners underserved and excluding essential career and life-skill development.

Goals, Objectives, and AIEE Solution Framework

AIEE aims to strengthen students’ decision-making capacity, reduce career and admission-related confusion, and develop confidence alongside practical, future-ready skills, ensuring an equity-focused nationwide impact. Its objectives include providing clear career direction, enhancing skill awareness, and delivering reliable, verified information for both students and parents through a mentor- and volunteer-driven guidance system. To achieve these goals, AIEE implements structured learning paths tailored to different educational stages, comprehensive mentor-led guidance, and community-based learning environments. The platform ensures accessibility through high-quality, low-bandwidth-friendly content, making effective guidance available to students across both urban and rural regions.

Core Activities, Mentor Framework, and Long-term Vision

AIEE operates through structured school and college outreach programs, including orientation sessions, career-focused workshops, and interactive activities to enhance student engagement. It delivers stage-wise structured learning paths—School Explorer, HSC Transition, University Starter, and Career & Skill Discovery—ensuring academic readiness, skill awareness, and career preparedness. A robust mentor-led guidance system integrates university professors, senior students, industry professionals, subject experts, and senior advisors to provide holistic academic, career, and life-skills support. AIEE’s long-term vision is to establish a nationwide structured guidance network that cultivates a skilled, confident, and informed generation while reducing the urban–rural education gap and preventing missed opportunities.
